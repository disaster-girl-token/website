Introduction


Disaster Girl Token (DISG), a modernized project, idealized by a group of students who saw the need to find a correlation of Information Technology, Blockchain and NFT (Non-Fungible-tokens) in this era of digitization.

Disaster Girl Token is the first concept in the Blockchain and digitization storm that will help improve NFT trading between different platforms through Blockchain via Silicon Valley concept.

Focusing mainly on Blockchain, Disaster Girl Token will offer its coin with IDO and ICO by linking with major platforms (Polka starter, Pancake swap, Uni swap) for trading in the cryptocurrency market. Aiming to bridge the gaps in information technology, Disaster Girl Token will focus on data science, data engineering, data visualization, NFTs and advanced automated formulas to make a revolution with respect to efficiency and effectiveness in the Blockchain and IT industry. The ultimate goal is to develop the technological valleys around the world.

Major Events


One month after the end of the pre-sale, 0.2% of DISG's max supply will be raffled among the holders through social networks.
